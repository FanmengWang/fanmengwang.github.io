About Me

🎓 I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate at Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence, Renmin University of China, under the supervision of Prof. Hongteng Xu. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from the School of Information Science and Engineering, Southeast University in June 2022.

🔬 My research focuses on data-driven machine learning, aiming to advance large language models, deep generative models, and geometric deep learning through novel neural network architectures and learning paradigms, exploring frontier applications in mathematical reasoning and scientific discovery.

✉️ Feel free to reach me for collaborations or discussions!
